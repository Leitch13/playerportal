## CONTENT BATCHING GUIDE

### How to Film a Month of Content in One Day

#### Pre-Production (1-2 hours, day before)

1. **Script all 8+ reels** in a Google Doc. Print them or have them on a tablet.
2. **Plan outfits**: Bring 3-4 different tops/jackets so content looks like different days.
3. **Charge everything**: Phone, camera (if using one), portable battery.
4. **Prep locations**: You need 3-4 settings:
   - On the pitch (empty, daylight)
   - In the car
   - At home / office
   - Walking somewhere (Aberdeen landmarks if possible)
5. **Create all carousel designs** in Canva the night before. Batch-design all 12+ carousels in one sitting.

#### Filming Day Schedule

**8:00 - 9:00 AM -- Home/Office Shots**
- Film any reels that are sit-down, talking head, intimate stories (Day 11 leap story, Day 14 reflection)
- Film in natural light near a window
- Change tops between takes
- Film 3-4 Story sequences against a simple background

**9:30 - 11:30 AM -- Pitch Shots**
- This is your main location. Film everything pitch-related:
  - Walking and talking reels (Day 1, Day 3, Day 17)
  - B-roll: setting up cones, walking the pitch, checking conditions
  - Static talking head pieces
  - Multiple angles: close-up, medium, wide
- Bring props: cones, bibs, ball bag, first aid kit
- Film extra 10-15 second clips of the pitch for story backgrounds

**11:30 - 12:00 PM -- Car/Driving Shots**
- Quick pieces filmed in the car (Day 11 alternative angle)
- "On my way to a session" style content
- B-roll of driving (PARKED, not while driving)

**12:00 - 12:30 PM -- Lunch Break**

**12:30 - 2:00 PM -- Remaining Content**
- Any remaining reels
- Selfie-style photos for single image posts
- Behind the scenes content for stories
- Film yourself reading comments / looking at phone for B-roll

**2:00 - 4:00 PM -- Editing & Scheduling**
- Edit reels in CapCut (free, easy, good subtitles)
- Add subtitles to every single reel (80% of people watch without sound)
- Schedule everything using Meta Business Suite (free) or Later
- Write all captions and hashtags, copy them into the scheduler
- Schedule stories in advance where possible

#### Equipment (Minimal)**
- Phone (that's it, really)
- Cheap phone tripod (15 quid from Amazon)
- Clip-on microphone if filming outdoors (20 quid -- worth it for wind)
- Ring light for indoor shots (optional, natural light is fine)

#### Batch Tips:
- Film ALL your close-ups first, then ALL your wide shots. Saves repositioning the tripod.
- Record reels as one continuous take even if you mess up -- just pause and restart. Edit out mistakes later.
- Film 5-10 extra "generic" clips each month: walking on the pitch, setting up equipment, coaching gestures. Use these as B-roll throughout the month.
- Keep a running list of content ideas on your phone. Every time you think "that would make a good post," write it down immediately.
- Batch your carousel creation separately -- Canva on a laptop is much faster than phone. Create a template once and duplicate it for every carousel.
