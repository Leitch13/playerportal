## Campaign Overview & Structure

### The Funnel

```
COLD (don't know John)
  → Awareness ads (story, problem, lead magnet)
    → WARM (visited page / engaged with content)
      → Consideration ads (proof, objections, behind the scenes)
        → HOT (downloaded the free guide)
          → Conversion ads (strategy call, urgency, case study)
```

### Campaign Architecture in Ads Manager

```
CAMPAIGN: PlayIt Love It Mentorship — Full Funnel
├── Ad Set 1: Cold — Awareness (Top of Funnel)
│   ├── Ad 1a/b/c: The Story
│   ├── Ad 2a/b/c: The Problem
│   └── Ad 3a/b/c: The Lead Magnet
├── Ad Set 2: Warm — Consideration (Middle of Funnel)
│   ├── Ad 4a/b/c: Social Proof
│   ├── Ad 5a/b/c: The Objection Killer
│   └── Ad 6a/b/c: Behind the Scenes
└── Ad Set 3: Hot — Conversion (Bottom of Funnel)
    ├── Ad 7a/b/c: The Offer
    ├── Ad 8a/b/c: Urgency
    └── Ad 9a/b/c: Case Study
```

|--------|-------------------|--------------|

## Budget Framework

### Starting Budget: £15/day (£450/month)

| Ad Set | % of Budget | Daily Spend | Monthly Spend |
|--------|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| Cold — Awareness | 60% | £9.00 | £270 |
| Warm — Consideration | 25% | £3.75 | £112.50 |
| Hot — Conversion | 15% | £2.25 | £67.50 |

**Why heavy on cold?** You need to fill the top of the funnel first. The warm and hot audiences build over time from cold traffic. After 2-3 weeks, you'll have enough retargeting data to increase spend on the lower funnel.

### Scaling Budget: £30/day (£900/month)

| Ad Set | % of Budget | Daily Spend | Monthly Spend |
|--------|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| Cold — Awareness | 50% | £15.00 | £450 |
| Warm — Consideration | 30% | £9.00 | £270 |
| Hot — Conversion | 20% | £6.00 | £180 |

## A/B Testing Strategy

### Phase 1: Copy Testing (Weeks 1-2)

Run all 3 primary text variations for each ad simultaneously. Keep the creative (image/video) the same. Let Meta's algorithm distribute spend to the best performer.

**Kill rule:** After 1,000 impressions per variation, pause any with a CTR below 0.8%. After £20 spend per variation, pause any with a cost per click above £2.

**Winner criteria:** Lowest cost per lead (for lead magnet ads) or cost per strategy call booking (for conversion ads).

### Phase 2: Creative Testing (Weeks 3-4)

Take the winning copy from Phase 1. Now test different creative formats:
- Static image vs. video
- Different thumbnail images
- Different video hooks (test 3-second hooks)
- Carousel vs. single image

**Kill rule:** Same as above. Give each creative at least £15-20 before making a call.

### Phase 3: Audience Testing (Weeks 5-6)

Take winning copy + creative. Now test audience variations:
- Interest stacking (Layer 1 only vs. Layer 1 + Layer 2)
- Age ranges (21-35 vs. 35-55 vs. broad)
- UK-wide vs. Scotland only vs. England only
- Lookalike audiences (once you have 100+ lead magnet downloaders)

### Phase 4: Scaling Winners (Week 7+)

You should now have 2-3 ads that clearly outperform. Scale these (see Scaling Playbook below).

### Ongoing Testing Rules

- Always have at least one test running alongside your proven winners
- Never test more than one variable at a time
- Document everything: what you tested, the result, the learning
- Refresh creative every 4-6 weeks to combat ad fatigue
- When an ad's frequency hits 3.0+, swap the creative

## Scaling Playbook

### When to Scale

Scale when you have an ad that:
- Has been running for at least 7 days
- Has a cost per lead below £5 (for lead magnet) or cost per strategy call below £30
- Has maintained consistent performance for 5+ days
- Has a frequency below 2.5

### How to Scale

**Method 1: Gradual Budget Increase (Safest)**
- Increase daily budget by no more than 20% every 3 days
- Example: £10/day → £12/day → £14.40/day → £17.28/day
- Monitor performance after each increase. If cost per lead jumps more than 30%, drop back to previous budget and wait 3 days.

**Method 2: Duplicate and Scale**
- Duplicate the winning ad set
- Set the duplicate to a higher budget
- Run both simultaneously for 3 days
- If the duplicate performs similarly, pause the original
- If the duplicate underperforms, pause it and stick with the original

**Method 3: Audience Expansion**
- Once a cold audience is performing well, create a Lookalike audience (1%) from your lead magnet downloaders
- Test the same winning ad with this new audience
- Lookalike audiences often outperform interest-based targeting once you have enough data (minimum 100 people in the source audience)

### Scaling Milestones

| Monthly Spend | What You Should See | Action |
|---------------|-------------------|--------|
| £450/mo (£15/day) | 50-100 leads/mo, 5-10 strategy calls | Validate funnel works |
| £900/mo (£30/day) | 100-200 leads/mo, 10-20 strategy calls | Start scaling winners |
| £1,500/mo (£50/day) | 200-350 leads/mo, 20-35 strategy calls | Full funnel optimisation |
| £3,000/mo (£100/day) | 400-700 leads/mo, 40-70 strategy calls | Advanced: lookalikes + new creative |

### Warning Signs (Pull Back If You See These)

- Cost per lead doubles overnight → creative fatigue, refresh the ad
- CTR drops below 0.5% → audience is saturated, expand targeting
- Strategy call no-show rate above 40% → lead quality issue, tighten targeting
- Frequency above 3.5 → same people seeing your ad too many times

## Key Metrics & Benchmarks

### Metrics to Track

| Metric | What It Tells You | Where to Find It |
|--------|-------------------|-----------------|
| CPM (Cost per 1,000 impressions) | How much you're paying for eyeballs | Ads Manager |
| CTR (Click-through rate) | How compelling your ad is | Ads Manager |
| CPC (Cost per click) | How much each click costs | Ads Manager |
| CPL (Cost per lead) | Cost to get a guide download | Ads Manager / Landing page |
| Strategy call booking rate | % of leads who book a call | Calendar tool / CRM |
| Call show-up rate | % who actually turn up | Calendar tool |
| Close rate | % of calls that convert to paying clients | Manual tracking |
| Customer acquisition cost (CAC) | Total ad spend / new clients | Manual calculation |
| Return on ad spend (ROAS) | Revenue from clients / ad spend | Manual calculation |

### Benchmark Targets (UK Coaching Niche)

| Metric | Poor | Acceptable | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|------------|------|-----------|
| CTR (link click) | <0.5% | 0.5-1.0% | 1.0-2.0% | >2.0% |
| CPC | >£3.00 | £1.50-3.00 | £0.80-1.50 | <£0.80 |
| CPL (lead magnet) | >£10 | £5-10 | £3-5 | <£3 |
| Landing page conversion | <15% | 15-25% | 25-40% | >40% |
| Strategy call booking rate | <3% | 3-5% | 5-10% | >10% |
| Call show-up rate | <50% | 50-65% | 65-80% | >80% |
| Close rate (on call) | <10% | 10-20% | 20-35% | >35% |

### The Numbers That Actually Matter

Forget vanity metrics. Here's what you need to track weekly:

1. **Cost per strategy call booked** — This is your north star. If calls cost less than 10% of your mentorship price, you're in great shape.
2. **Show-up rate** — Below 60%? Add SMS reminders, a confirmation email sequence, and a "what to expect" video.
3. **Close rate** — Below 15%? The issue is likely lead quality (tighten your targeting) or your call script (separate problem to solve).
4. **ROAS** — If you're charging £2,000 for mentorship and spending £200 to get a client, that's 10x ROAS. Anything above 5x = scale aggressively.

### Weekly Review Checklist

Every Monday, check:
- [ ] Total spend this week vs. budget
- [ ] Number of leads generated
- [ ] Cost per lead trend (up, down, stable?)
- [ ] Number of strategy calls booked
- [ ] Number of calls that happened (show-up rate)
- [ ] Any calls converted to clients?
- [ ] Which ads are performing best? Which should be paused?
- [ ] Frequency: any ads above 3.0?
- [ ] Any creative due for refresh?

## Final Notes

**This is a marathon, not a sprint.** The first 2-3 weeks are about gathering data, not making sales. Don't panic if leads are expensive at first. The algorithm needs time to learn.

**Authenticity is your competitive advantage.** You're not a guru. You're not selling a dream. You're a coach who built something real and is willing to show others how. Every piece of copy should reflect that. If it sounds like it could come from any random business coach on Instagram, rewrite it.

**Video outperforms static almost always** in this niche. Football coaches want to see a real person, not a graphic. Invest time in getting comfortable on camera. It doesn't need to be polished — it needs to be real.

**Start with Ad Set 1 only.** Run cold traffic for 2 weeks to build your retargeting audiences before turning on Ad Sets 2 and 3. There's no point retargeting people if you haven't built up an audience to retarget.

**The lead magnet is the engine.** If your lead magnet conversion rate is below 25%, fix the landing page before spending more on ads. No amount of good ad copy will fix a poor landing page.
