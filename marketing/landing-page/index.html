The real story

My Journey

In 2018, I started running football sessions in Aberdeen with a simple belief: if you coach well and genuinely care about the kids, people will come. I didn't have a business plan. I didn't have a marketing strategy. I had a bag of balls and a local pitch.

The first year was a grind. I did everything myself—coaching, admin, social media, chasing invoices. There were weeks where I questioned if this could ever be more than a side hustle.

But I kept showing up. I learned what worked and what didn't. I figured out how to get parents talking, how to price sessions so I wasn't working for free, and how to build systems so I wasn't drowning in admin.

Fast-forward to today: PlayIt Love It works with over 300 players every single week. I've built a team of coaches, multiple programmes, and a business that gives me the life I wanted. Now I help other coaches do the same thing.
